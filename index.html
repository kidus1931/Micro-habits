The power of micro-habits:
small habits that transform your life

Have you ever set big, shiny goal-like runningn a marathon, writing a book, or getting organized- only to crash and burn a week later? you're not alone. Big goals can feel like mountains, and the climb often seems too steep. but what if i told you that you could transform your life with changes so small they almost feel effortless?

Enter micro-habits: tiny, intentional actions that take less than a minute or two but compound over time to create massive results.
Imagine writing just one sentence a day. In a year, you'd have 365 sentences-enough for short story or the start of a novel.
Or doing one push-up daily. It sounds silly, but it could spark a fitness routine.
